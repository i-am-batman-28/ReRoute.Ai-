1. PROBLEM WE'RE SOLVING
================================================================================

In India's public health system:
- PHCs (Primary Health Centers) refer patients to tertiary hospitals for
  specialist care, but there is NO unified digital system to track this.
- Referrals happen on paper slips, phone calls, or WhatsApp messages.
- Hospitals often don't know a referral is coming.
- PHCs have no idea whether the patient reached, was treated, or what happened.
- Counter-referrals (hospital sending patient back to PHC with follow-up plan)
  almost never happen properly.
- Result: patients get LOST between levels of care. Delayed treatment,
  repeated tests, no continuity.

Key stat (for pitch): Studies show 30–70% of referrals in low-resource
settings have no documented outcome. Patients fall through the cracks.


2. OUR SOLUTION — "ReferSync"
================================================================================

A single digital platform that connects PHC workers and hospital staff so
every referral is tracked, every counter-referral is received, and no
patient is lost between levels of care.

Three core pillars:
  (a) SMART REFERRAL   — PHC creates and sends referral with AI-assisted
                          department suggestion and urgency scoring.
  (b) LIVE TRACKING    — Both PHC and hospital see real-time status of
                          every referral on a shared timeline.
  (c) COUNTER-REFERRAL — Hospital sends structured handover back to PHC
                          with AI-drafted summary, follow-up plan, and meds.


3. USER ROLES
================================================================================

Role 1: PHC HEALTH WORKER (creates referrals, receives counter-referrals)
Role 2: HOSPITAL STAFF (receives referrals, updates status, sends counter-referrals)
Role 3: ADMIN / DISTRICT OFFICER (optional — dashboard view of all referrals
        across PHCs and hospitals for monitoring)


4. FULL USER FLOW (step by step)
================================================================================

--- PHC SIDE ---

Step 1: PHC worker logs in (simple login: phone number + OTP or ID + password).

Step 2: Taps "New Referral."

Step 3: Fills referral form:
        - Patient name, age, gender, contact number
        - Aadhaar or health ID (optional, for tracking)
        - Reason for referral (free text + common presets like
          "Chest pain," "Pregnancy complications," "Fracture," etc.)
        - Current symptoms and vitals (BP, temp, pulse — optional)
        - Brief history (free text)
        - Attachments (photo of reports, prescriptions — optional)

Step 4: AI SUGGESTION kicks in:
        → Based on the reason + symptoms, the system suggests:
          - Recommended DEPARTMENT (e.g. Cardiology, Orthopedics, OB-GYN)
          - Suggested URGENCY (Critical / Urgent / Routine)
          - Nearest HOSPITAL that has this department (if multiple hospitals
            are in the system)
        → PHC worker can accept or override the suggestion.

Step 5: PHC worker taps "Send Referral."
        → Referral gets a unique ID: e.g. REF-2026-0342
        → Status: SENT
        → Hospital receives a notification (in-app + optional SMS/WhatsApp).

Step 6: PHC worker can see the referral in "My Referrals" list with
        live status updates.

--- HOSPITAL SIDE ---

Step 7: Hospital staff logs in. Sees "Incoming Referrals" inbox.

Step 8: Opens the referral. Sees all details + AI-suggested department.
        Taps "Accept" (or "Redirect" to a different department).
        → Status updates to: ACCEPTED
        → PHC sees this update in real time.

Step 9: When patient physically arrives, hospital taps "Patient Arrived."
        → Status: PATIENT ARRIVED
        → PHC sees this.

Step 10: During treatment, hospital can optionally add notes
         (diagnosis, tests done, treatment given).

Step 11: When patient is ready for discharge / follow-up at PHC,
         hospital taps "Prepare Counter-Referral."

Step 12: COUNTER-REFERRAL FORM (AI-assisted):
         → System auto-drafts a summary from referral reason +
           hospital notes using an LLM (e.g. GPT API or open-source).
         → Hospital staff reviews and edits:
           - Diagnosis
           - Treatment given
           - Medications prescribed
           - Follow-up instructions for PHC
           - Next appointment date (if any)
           - Red flags to watch for
         → Taps "Send Counter-Referral."
         → Status: COUNTER-REFERRED
         → PHC receives notification.

--- BACK TO PHC ---

Step 13: PHC worker opens the referral → sees full journey:
         SENT → ACCEPTED → PATIENT ARRIVED → COUNTER-REFERRED

Step 14: Reads the counter-referral:
         - What the hospital found and did
         - What meds the patient is on
         - What PHC should monitor
         - When to send the patient back if needed

Step 15: PHC worker taps "Acknowledged" → referral is marked COMPLETE.

--- FULL CYCLE DONE ---


5. KEY FEATURES (what makes it a WINNER)
================================================================================

Feature 1: AI-POWERED REFERRAL SUGGESTION
  - When PHC worker types reason + symptoms, system suggests:
    → Department (from a trained classifier or rule set + LLM)
    → Urgency level (Critical / Urgent / Routine)
    → Nearest suitable hospital
  - This is the "smart" in "Smart Referral."
  - Tech: Simple classifier trained on common symptom→department mappings,
    or LLM prompt: "Given these symptoms, suggest department and urgency."

Feature 2: REAL-TIME STATUS TIMELINE
  - Every referral has a visual timeline:
    [Sent] → [Accepted] → [Patient Arrived] → [Counter-Referred] → [Complete]
  - Both PHC and hospital see the SAME timeline.
  - Updates appear in near real-time (WebSocket or polling).
  - This is the "tracking" the brief asks for.

Feature 3: AI-DRAFTED COUNTER-REFERRAL SUMMARY
  - Hospital taps "Prepare Counter-Referral" → LLM drafts a structured
    summary from referral details + hospital notes.
  - Hospital staff reviews, edits, sends.
  - PHC gets a clear, readable handover — not messy handwriting.
  - This is the second "AI" moment.

Feature 4: NOTIFICATION SYSTEM
  - When referral is sent → hospital gets notified.
  - When status changes → PHC gets notified.
  - When counter-referral arrives → PHC gets notified.
  - In-app notifications + optional SMS (using Twilio or similar).

Feature 5: REFERRAL DASHBOARD (for both sides + admin)
  - PHC dashboard: My referrals, pending, accepted, counter-referred,
    average response time.
  - Hospital dashboard: Incoming, in-progress, completed, busiest
    departments, referral volume trends.
  - Admin/district dashboard: All referrals across PHCs and hospitals,
    referral completion rate, average turnaround, bottlenecks.
  - Shows judges you think about SCALE and MONITORING.

Feature 6: ONE REFERRAL ID, FULL TRACEABILITY
  - Every referral has a unique ID (e.g. REF-2026-0342).
  - Patient, PHC, hospital, and admin can trace any referral by this ID.
  - Full audit log: who did what, when.

Feature 7: MULTI-LANGUAGE SUPPORT (bonus)
  - PHC workers in rural areas may prefer Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, etc.
  - At minimum: English + one regional language for the UI.
  - Shows inclusivity and real-world thinking.

8. DATABASE SCHEMA (simplified)
================================================================================

USERS
  - id, name, role (phc_worker / hospital_staff / admin), facility_id,
    phone, password_hash

FACILITIES
  - id, name, type (phc / tertiary_hospital), location, departments[]

REFERRALS
  - id (REF-2026-XXXX)
  - patient_name, patient_age, patient_gender, patient_contact
  - patient_health_id (optional)
  - reason, symptoms, vitals, history
  - suggested_department, suggested_urgency (AI-generated)
  - chosen_department, chosen_urgency (PHC worker's final choice)
  - from_facility_id (PHC)
  - to_facility_id (Hospital)
  - created_by (PHC worker user id)
  - status: SENT | ACCEPTED | PATIENT_ARRIVED | DISCHARGED |
            COUNTER_REFERRED | COMPLETE
  - created_at, updated_at

REFERRAL_EVENTS (audit log / timeline)
  - id, referral_id, event_type, changed_by, timestamp, notes

COUNTER_REFERRALS
  - id, referral_id
  - diagnosis, treatment_given, medications, follow_up_instructions
  - next_appointment_date, red_flags
  - ai_draft (what LLM generated)
  - final_summary (what hospital staff sent)
  - created_by (hospital staff user id)
  - created_at


9. AI / ML COMPONENTS
================================================================================

AI Component 1: REFERRAL SUGGESTION ENGINE
  Input:  Reason text + symptoms
  Output: Suggested department + urgency level
  How:    Prompt an LLM:
          "Given these symptoms: [symptoms], suggest the most appropriate
           hospital department and urgency level (Critical/Urgent/Routine).
           Respond in JSON: {department, urgency, reasoning}"
          OR: Simple rule-based classifier (symptom keywords → department)
          OR: Small trained model on symptom→department dataset

AI Component 2: COUNTER-REFERRAL SUMMARY GENERATOR
  Input:  Referral details + hospital notes
  Output: Structured summary (diagnosis, treatment, follow-up)
  How:    Prompt an LLM:
          "Based on this referral [details] and hospital notes [notes],
           draft a counter-referral summary with: diagnosis, treatment given,
           medications, follow-up instructions, and red flags."
          Hospital staff reviews and edits before sending.

AI Component 3 (bonus): REFERRAL ANALYTICS
  Input:  Historical referral data
  Output: Insights: "PHC X has high referral rate for cardiology;
           consider adding a cardiologist visit schedule"
  How:    Simple aggregation + LLM-generated insight text.
